A Little About Myself

I'm currently a student of the Thinkful Software Engineering Immersion Program. From a young age, I've always been drawn to different forms of creation. At one point in my early twenties, I overheard a friend of mine say "Have you ever noticed that he's just always doing something? "
Whether it be related to music and art (my first loves) or being able to assemble something with my own two hands, like a computer, I quite simply just love to make stuff.
As technology and myself have both grown and changed, I've found myself interested in it more every day, which led me to pursue a career in software engineering, to combine my desire to create with an ever-growing understanding of the world of code.
Though my roots are largely in being self-taught and autonomous, I aim to improve myself every day through guidance and teamwork to become both more dynamic as a developer and more synergistic as a collaborator. When I'm not coding, you can usually find me still on my laptop, working on a new song, or trying my hand at another new skill.

A Little Of My Work (So Far)

Stardew Valley Quiz Application

This is a short quiz application based on the open-ended RPG and farm-simulator Stardew Valley, made to put fans of all skill level's knowledge of the game's story to the test and provide a pleasant experience while doing so. Made in collaboration with Thomas Davis for Thinkful's Software Engineering Immersion Program.

Built with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.
