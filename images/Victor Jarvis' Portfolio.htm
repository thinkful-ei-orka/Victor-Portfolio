A Little About Myself

I'm currently a student of the Thinkful Software Engineering Immersion Program. From a young age, I've always been drawn to different forms of creation. At one point in my early twenties, I overheard a friend of mine say "Have you ever noticed that he's just always doing something? "

Whether it be rooted in music and art (my first loves) or being able to assemble something with my own two hands, like a computer, I quite simply just love to make stuff.

As technology and myself have both grown and changed, I've found myself interested in it more every day, which led me to pursue a career in software engineering, to combine my desire to create with an ever-growing understanding of the world of code. When I'm not coding, you can usually find me still on my laptop, working on a new song, or trying my hand at another new skill.
